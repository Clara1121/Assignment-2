Project Moon has made three games that I have played and enjoyed. Their games in order of my favourite are:

  1. Library of Runia
  2. Limbus Company
  3. Lobotomy Cotporattion

Each of these games have dark themes that deal with the idea of a corrupt world where even people who are at the top have their own struggles. The gameplay for each game is usually simple form managment simulator to deck beckbuilding.These games are some of my favourite because of the wide range of characters with in dept story telling and world building.All these games take place in a city and the story of each game connects.
